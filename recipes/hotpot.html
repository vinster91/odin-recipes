Delicious Hotpot Recipe

Below is a description on how Chinese Hotpot can be prepared under 90 minutes, with a prep time of 90min and serving 12 people.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb beef flank
  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 1 lb pork chop
  • 2 lb tilapia fish fillets
  • 1.5 lb shrimp (size 31-35)
  • 4 oz dried vermicelli noodles

Steps

  1. Freeze and partially thaw meat
  2. Wash & prepare any vegetables, mushrooms
  3. Cut beef, pork, chicken into thin slices
  4. Prepare fish balls
  5. Prepare soup base
